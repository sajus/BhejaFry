Interactive charts

Cybage UI - IMS provide reports in the form of interactive charts that shows statistics for interviews conducted.

  • You can filter the charts data by legend provided on chart panel.
  • Charts are available for download in PNG, JPEG, SVG or PDF file formats.
  • Charts are also available for printing perpose.

Reports

The following reports are included with Cybage UI - IMS:

  • Interview's overall report: Shows overall interview conducted status, grouped by interviewed status, mode or round.
  • Interviewer overall report: Shows overall interviews conducted filtered by interviewers.

Managing your account settings

We highly recommended to periodically change your password. Now you can do it easily with change password facility provided.

  • If your password isn't working, you'll need to go through our password recovery process by raising a reset request to administrator.
  • If your account is locked, you'll need to go through our account recovery process by raising an unlock request to administrator.
